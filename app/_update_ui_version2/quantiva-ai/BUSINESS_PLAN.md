## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### 1.1 Business Concept

Quantiva AI is an AI-powered regulatory document intelligence platform that enables businesses to generate, customize, and manage compliance documents in minutes rather than weeks. By leveraging cutting-edge artificial intelligence (GPT-4), we transform the $67 billion regulatory compliance software market by democratizing access to high-quality legal documentation for SMEs and enterprises worldwide.

### 1.2 The Problem

Organizations today face unprecedented regulatory complexity:
- **Time:** Average 40+ hours to create a single compliance document
- **Cost:** £5,000-£50,000 per document for legal review
- **Complexity:** 75,000+ international regulations across 190+ countries
- **Change:** GDPR, DORA, CCPA, and other frameworks evolve constantly
- **Access:** 41% of product recalls linked to compliance failures

### 1.3 The Solution

Quantiva AI provides a comprehensive platform that delivers:
- **AI Document Generation:** Create customized compliance documents in under 5 minutes
- **Template Library:** 10,000+ pre-vetted templates across 50+ jurisdictions
- **Real-time Updates:** AI-powered monitoring of regulatory changes
- **Multi-jurisdiction Support:** Compliance for UK, EU, US, APAC markets
- **Collaborative Workspace:** Team-based document management
- **Browser Extension:** Generate documents directly from web content
